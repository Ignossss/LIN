# Intro
1. Enjoy yourself
	Contrary to how he desires to perceive himself, man still is an animal, concealing this truth with terms such as 'emotion.' With his imperfect intelligence, he still places himself above other creatures - forgetting the fact that he is also one of them!
	
	Man, as an animal, enjoys recreation, desiring day, and night in accord with his nature. But there exist individuals who are unable to enjoy themselves due to limitations or their inabilities, developing contempt for those who can; Their harmful ideas eventually has spread to many people who otherwise were very recreational.
	
	But there must be no shame in indulgence, for it is all natural, and essential for a healthy body, and free mind. On the other hand, compulsion, which is created either by inability, or unwillingness to indulge, only disturbs the individual.

# Rules
## Executive
All members must be equal.
All actions affecting the majority of members must be voted on and approved by majority.
All disputes may be resolved by founding head's descision.
## Membership
1. membership requirements:
	Signatory must be over the age of 20;
	Signatory must be known by the founding head;
	Signatory must be an nonreligious;
	Signatory must have at least one useful artistic skill;
	Signatory must be approved by the founding head.
2. membership rules:
	Members must keep LUA's a secret;
	Members must respect others' freedoms;
	Members must participate at least in one meeting each 2 weeks;
	Members must finish given projects each month;
	Members must exchange knowledge of artistic capabilities;
The membership is voided immediately after failure to meet any criteria.
3. Member rights of freedom:
	Complete freedom of speech;
	Complete freedom of ideologies; And
	complete freedom of expression.
